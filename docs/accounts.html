Account Analytics

Know which accounts perform

SOLUS scores every account based on order history and assigns performance tiers so you can make data-driven decisions.

🏆

Tier System

Accounts are ranked into four tiers based on stick rate: Perfect (≥80%), Good (60–79%), Okay (40–59%), and At Risk (<40%).

📈

Per-Account Stick Rate

See the exact stick rate, score, and total order count for every individual account. No more guessing which ones are working.

🏠

Address Grouping

Group accounts by address or profile to see aggregate stats at the household level. Identify which addresses get flagged.

🔧

Advanced Filtering

Filter by tier, date range, minimum stick rate, and minimum order count. Narrow down to exactly the accounts you care about.

🗃

Grid & List View

Export Perfect Accounts

Tiers

Performance at a glance

The tier system gives you an instant read on account health. Each tier has a distinct color so you can scan your account list and spot problems immediately.

  • Perfect (≥80%) — your best accounts
  • Good (60–79%) — solid performers
  • Okay (40–59%) — needs attention
  • At Risk (<40%) — consider retiring
🏆
Profiles

Group by address for the big picture

Multiple accounts at the same address? SOLUS groups them into profiles so you can see combined performance and identify addresses with consistently high or low stick rates.

  • Automatic address detection and grouping
  • Profile-level stick rate and order count
  • Expand profiles to see individual accounts
  • Sort profiles by performance
