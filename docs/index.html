Features

Everything you need to manage orders

SOLUS connects to your email and automatically pulls in order data from major retailers, giving you a unified view of all your purchases.

📦

Multi-Retailer Tracking

Automatically parse and track orders from Walmart, Target, Amazon, Best Buy, Costco, Sam's Club, and Pokemon Center.

📊

Spending Analytics

Interactive charts and breakdowns of your spending by retailer, category, and time period. Know exactly where your money goes.

🚚

Live Delivery Tracking

Real-time delivery status updates with estimated arrival dates. Never miss a package again.

📅

Delivery Calendar

Visual calendar view showing when your orders are arriving. Plan your week around your deliveries.

🔒

Privacy First

Your data stays on your machine. SOLUS connects directly to your email over IMAP — no middleman servers, no cloud storage.

📣

Discord Integration

Get instant order and delivery notifications pushed to your Discord server via webhooks.
