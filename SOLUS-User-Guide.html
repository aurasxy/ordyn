Section 01

Getting Started

Set up SOLUS for the first time and learn the basics of navigating the app.

Activating Your License

1

Enter Your License Key

When you first open SOLUS, you'll be prompted to enter your license key in the format XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX. Click Activate.

2

Verification

SOLUS verifies your license online. Once activated, your plan type, status, and expiration date appear under Settings > License.

Tip

Your license can be deactivated and moved to another machine from Settings > License > Disconnect.

Choosing a Data Mode

SOLUS supports two data sources. Pick the one that matches your setup:

IMAP Mode (Email Sync)

Connect your Gmail, Yahoo, Outlook, or iCloud email accounts. SOLUS reads order confirmation emails directly via IMAP to track orders automatically.

Best for: Users who receive all order confirmations by email.

Discord Mode (ACO Bot)

Link your Discord account via the SOLUS bot. Orders are captured from ACO bot embeds posted in your Discord channels.

Best for: Users running automated checkout bots (Shikari, Stellara, etc.) that post results to Discord.

Switch between modes any time in Settings > General > Data Mode. Only one mode is active at a time.

Navigation

The left sidebar contains all main pages:

  • Dashboard — Overview of all orders with stats and quick-glance metrics
  • Deliveries — Track shipments, view calendar, live FedEx tracking
  • Analytics — Charts, trends, patterns, and insights across your orders
  • Account Stats — Per-account/profile stick rates and performance tiers
  • IMAP — Manage email accounts, run syncs, view logs
  • Discord — Link Discord, configure webhooks, sync ACO orders
  • Reports — Generate and export PDF/CSV/PNG reports
  • Inventory — Track purchased products, log sales, calculate profit
  • TCG Tracker — Monitor TCGPlayer card prices over time
  • Settings — Preferences, license, data management, address grouping

Below the main pages, retailer-specific order pages show orders for each supported retailer:

Walmart   Target   Pokemon Center   Sam's Club   Costco   Best Buy

Section 02

Dashboard

Your home screen with a real-time snapshot of all order activity.

Retailer Cards

Six cards at the top show per-retailer order counts broken down by status:

  • Confirmed Orders placed successfully
  • Cancelled Orders that were cancelled
  • Shipped Orders in transit
  • Delivered Orders that have arrived

Click any retailer card to jump to that retailer's order page. Toggle cards on/off via the customize button.

Overall Stats

Six summary cards show totals across all retailers: Confirmed, Cancelled, Shipped, Delivered, Total Orders, and Total Spent.

Filters

Period Filter

Show orders from the Last 7 Days, 30 Days, 90 Days, or All Time.

Source Filter

Toggle between All Sources, Email only, or Discord only.

Quick Stats Bar

A fixed bar at the bottom shows four key metrics. Click any to navigate to the relevant view:

  • Packages In Transit — Count of all shipped orders
  • Arriving Today — Orders expected today
  • This Month Spending — Total spent this calendar month
  • Delivered This Week — Orders delivered in the last 7 days
Section 03

Deliveries

Track every shipment from dispatch to doorstep with four powerful views.

In Transit

The default tab shows all currently shipping orders. Four summary cards at the top show counts for Today, This Week, In Transit, and Alerts (delayed shipments). Click a card to filter.

Views

  • Timeline — Grouped by delivery date (Today, Tomorrow, This Week, Later, No ETA)
  • By Address — Grouped by shipping address with search
  • By Retailer — Grouped by retailer

Delivery Cards

Each card shows product image, item name, address, carrier, and ETA with action buttons:

  • Track — Opens the carrier's tracking page
  • Mark Delivered — Manually mark as delivered
  • Expand — Full order details (order ID, amount, tracking, carrier, etc.)

Bulk Actions

Select multiple deliveries with checkboxes, then mark them all as delivered at once via the bulk bar.

Recently Delivered

All delivered orders, filterable by Last 7 Days, 30 Days, 90 Days, Last Year, or All Time.

Calendar

Monthly calendar view with deliveries on their expected arrival dates. Navigate months with arrows. Each day shows up to 3 items with a "+X more" indicator. Click any day for a full order popup.

Live Track (FedEx)

Real-time FedEx tracking showing current status, last known location, ETA, and full event timeline.

  • Status Filters — In Transit, Out for Delivery, Delivered, Exception, Pending, No Data
  • Date Range — Filter by expected delivery date
  • Refresh All / Clear Cache — Force-refresh or clear cached data
Section 04

Analytics

Deep insights into your order performance with charts, trends, and data-driven analysis.

Filters & Summary

  • Retailer — All, Walmart, Target, Pokemon Center, Sam's Club, Costco, Best Buy
  • Period — 7 Days, 30 Days, 90 Days, 1 Year, All Time
  • Compare — Overlay the previous period for side-by-side comparison

Summary cards show: Total Orders, Confirmed, Total Spent, and Stick Rate.

Overview Tab

Four interactive Chart.js charts:

  • Spending Trend — Daily spend over time (line chart)
  • Stick Rate Trend — Daily stick rate percentage (line chart)
  • Cancel Rate Trend — Daily cancellation rate (line chart)
  • Order Volume — Confirmed vs cancelled per day (stacked bar)

When Compare is on, each chart overlays the previous period as a secondary dataset.

Trends Tab

Retailer Comparison

Horizontal bar chart comparing retailers side by side. Toggle metric: Stick Rate, Orders, or Spending. Only visible when "All" retailers is selected.

Patterns Tab

Day-of-week bar chart showing which days you place the most orders. Filter by retailer. Includes generated insights about your ordering patterns.

Carrier Performance

Doughnut chart of carrier distribution + bar chart of average delivery days per carrier. Stats table shows shipment counts and averages.

Insights Tab

Five auto-generated insight cards:

  • Top Stick Rate — Highest confirmation rate items (min 3 orders)
  • Most Spent On — Items with the highest total spending
  • Top by Volume — Most frequently ordered items
  • Worst Stick Rate — Lowest confirmation rate items
  • Biggest Drop Days — Days with the largest performance shifts

Click any item name to filter analytics to that product.

Section 05

Account Stats

Per-account performance breakdown with stick rates, scoring, and tier rankings.

Key Metrics

Stick Rate

Stick Rate = Confirmed / (Total Orders - Manual Cancels) x 100

Manual cancels and quantity limit cancels are excluded from the denominator. Policy review, payment failures, and fraud detections still count against stick rate.

Performance Tiers

TierStick Rate
Perfect80% or higher
Good60 – 79%
Okay40 – 59%
At RiskBelow 40%

Filters & Controls

  • Retailer — Walmart, Target, Pokemon Center, Sam's Club, Costco, Best Buy
  • Period — 7 Days, 30 Days, 90 Days, All Time
  • Search — Search by email, address, or profile name
  • Sort — Stick Rate, Score, Order Count, Total Spent, Account Name, Address
  • View — Toggle between Grid and List layouts
  • Advanced Filter — Filter by performance tier and custom date range

Export Perfect Accounts

Click Export to save a .txt file listing all accounts with 80%+ stick rate. Respects current retailer and period filters. When "All" is selected, accounts are organized by site.

Pokemon Center: Address Grouping

Pokemon Center orders are grouped by base address instead of email. SOLUS normalizes addresses using built-in leetspeak patterns and doubled letter removal to group variants under one profile. Expand any address to see all variants and their orders.

Address nicknames set in Deliveries > By Address are also displayed on Account Stats profiles, making it easy to identify grouped addresses at a glance.

Discord Mode: Profile Stats

In Discord mode, accounts are shown as profiles with additional metrics:

  • Health Score — 0-100 composite score (stick rate - decline penalty + volume bonus)
  • Decline Rate — Percentage of payment-related failures
  • Recommendation — Performing Well, Needs Attention, or Consider Retiring

Connect your email accounts and sync order confirmations automatically.

Click "+ Add Account"

Syncing Orders

  • Sync (per account) — Sync a single account for the selected date range
  • Sync All — Sync all accounts concurrently
  • Sync Recent — Quick sync of the last 7 days across all accounts
  • Rescue from Spam — Search spam folders for missed order emails
  • Refresh Images — Re-match product names to local SKU images

Each account shows a progress bar with percentage and status. Use pause and stop buttons to control active syncs.

Pausing saves your place. Click Resume to continue later. SOLUS can auto-resume paused syncs after a configurable delay (set in Settings).

Sync Log

Click the log icon on any account for timestamped entries. Color-coded: green (success), yellow (warning), red (error). Updates in real time during sync.

Account Management

  • Nicknames — Custom label for easy identification
  • Edit Password — Update the app password
  • Test Connection — Verify credentials without a full sync
  • Delete Account — Removes account and all its synced orders
Section 07

Discord Integration

Connect SOLUS to your Discord server for ACO bot order tracking.

Linking Your Account

1

Invite the Bot

Click "Invite Discord Bot to Server". You need Manage Server permission.

2

Generate a Link Token

In SOLUS, click "Generate Link Token" to get a one-time code.

3

Link in Discord

Run /link <token> in any channel in your server. The bot confirms the link.

4

Watch Channels

Run /watch in each channel where your ACO bot posts. Optionally specify the bot type or let SOLUS auto-detect.

Syncing Discord Orders

  • Sync Now — Immediately pull all new orders from Discord
  • Auto-Sync — Automatic syncing at intervals (5 min, 15 min, 30 min, 1 hour)
  • Refresh Images — Re-link product images to local SKU mappings
  • Clear Discord Orders — Remove all Discord-synced orders (email orders unaffected)

Discord Webhook

Configure a Discord webhook URL in Settings > Reports to send generated reports directly to a Discord channel. Enter the URL, save, and test.

Important

Switch to Discord Mode in Settings > General to use Discord sync. While in IMAP mode, Discord sync is disabled.

Orders

View Modes

  • List View — Sortable table with configurable columns (Item, Account, ETA, Address, Track)

Tabs: All, Confirmed, Shipped, Delivered, Cancelled, Advanced. Full-text search across item, order ID, email, address, and tracking number.

Cancel Reasons

Non-Penalizing

Section 10

Inventory

Track purchased products, log sales, and calculate profit.

Portfolio Summary

  • Total Items — Number of unique products
  • Total Investment — Sum of (quantity x cost)
  • Market Value — Sum of (quantity x current market price)
  • Unrealized P/L — Market value minus investment (green = profit, red = loss)

Adding Items

Manual Add

Click "+ Add Item", paste a TCGPlayer URL, click Fetch. SOLUS scrapes the product image, name, set, and market price. Enter quantity and cost per item.

Import from Orders

Click "Import" to bulk-add from confirmed orders. Highlights items already in inventory. Use "Select New Only" to grab just the new ones.

Item Cards

Toggle Grid and List views. Search by name and sort by Name, Quantity, Investment, or Recently Added. Hover for quick actions (Log Sale, Delete).

Item Detail Modal

Click any item to open its full detail view:

  • Product image, name, set, quantity, cost, market value, profit/loss
  • Price history chart (line graph with area fill)
  • Linked drop info (which retailer drop it came from)
  • Sales history for this item
  • Actions: Log Sale, Refresh Price, Edit, Delete

Logging Sales

Log a sale from the item detail, the card hover button, or the Sales History tab. Enter:

  • Quantity sold, Sale price per unit
  • Platform — eBay, TCGPlayer, Mercari, Facebook Marketplace, Local, Other
  • Notes (optional)

Profit is calculated automatically (sale total minus cost basis). Inventory quantity decreases accordingly. If quantity reaches zero, the item is removed.

Sales History Tab

View all logged sales with summary cards: Items Sold, Revenue, Profit, Avg Margin. Filter by Platform and Date Range. Edit or delete any sale. Export to CSV.

Price Refresh

  • Single item — Refresh button in item detail
  • Refresh All — Update all inventory prices at once
  • Auto-Refresh — Set an interval to refresh prices automatically in the background

Proxy Configuration

If TCGPlayer blocks requests, configure proxy lists. Format: ip:port or ip:port:user:pass. Create named lists, select an active one, and test before using.

A lightweight price watchlist separate from Inventory. Track market prices for cards you're interested in buying or speculating on.

Section 13

Discord Bot

The SOLUS bot monitors ACO channels and syncs orders to the app in real time.

Bot Commands

CommandDescription
/link <token>Link Discord to SOLUS using a token from the app or your full license key.
/unlinkDisconnect Discord. Deactivates watched channels. Order history preserved.
/watch [channel] [bot]Start monitoring a channel for ACO orders. Auto-detects bot type or specify manually.
/unwatch [channel]Stop monitoring a channel.
/statusView link status, watched channels, total orders, and last 24h activity.
/scan [limit]Scan channel history for orders. Shows drop analytics with per-item breakdowns.
/theme [accent]Customize analytics card appearance. 6 themes, 16+ accent colors, custom hex.
/forward [webhook]Auto-forward parsed orders to a webhook in real time. Disable with /forward off.

Supported ACO Bots

BotDetectionNotes
ShikariAuto (footer)Primary supported bot. Field-based embed format.
StellaraAuto (footer)Grid-style fields. Multicart support. Filters non-order embeds.
TohruAuto (footer)Shikari-compatible format.
KodaiAuto (footer)Shikari-compatible format.
RefractAuto (footer)Title-based status ("Successful/Failed Checkout").
GenericFallbackDetects status from keywords and embed color. Works with most bots.

How Monitoring Works

The bot listens for new messages in watched channels. When an ACO embed is detected, SOLUS:

  • Detects the ACO bot type from the embed footer/author
  • Parses all fields: retailer, order ID, item, price, quantity, profile, email, status, decline reason
  • Queues the order for database storage (batched every 5 seconds)
  • Forwards to your webhook if configured

Multiple embeds per message and forwarded messages are all handled.

Data Extracted Per Order

  • Retailer — Site name
  • Order ID — Confirmation number
  • Item — Product name(s)
  • Amount — Price paid
  • Quantity — Units ordered
  • Status — Confirmed or Cancelled
  • Profile — Checkout profile name
  • Email — Account (password stripped)
  • Decline Reason — Failure reason
  • Image — Product thumbnail
  • ACO Bot — Source bot type
  • Date — When posted

Decline Reason Extraction

The bot extracts reasons from multiple sources in priority order:

  • Explicit fields: "Decline Reason", "Reason", "Cancel Reason"
  • Fuzzy field search: any field containing "reason", "decline", or "cancel"
  • Embed title: text after colon (e.g., "Order Canceled: Quantity Limit")
  • Fallback: embed description if under 200 characters

Non-penalizing reasons (quantity limit, manual cancel) don't affect stick rate in the SOLUS app.

Scan & Analytics

/scan analyzes channel history, grouping orders into drops (same retailer + date). Each drop shows total orders, confirmed, cancelled, success rate, and per-item breakdown with stick rates.

After viewing, you can:

  • Export to SOLUS — Write orders to the database
  • Post to Discord — Share analytics card image in channel
  • Save Image — Download analytics card as PNG

Themes

Customize analytics card appearance with /theme:

  • 6 Base Themes: Midnight, Ocean, Purple, Crimson, Emerald, Slate
  • 16+ Accent Colors: Green, Cyan, Blue, Purple, Pink, Red, Orange, Gold, and more
  • Custom Hex: Enter any #RRGGBB color

Live preview renders instantly before committing.

Webhook Forwarding

/forward <webhook_url> auto-forwards every parsed order to a Discord channel. Each embed shows status, decline reason, retailer, items, profile, and product image. Set a custom title or disable with /forward off.

Bot Permissions Required

Read Messages, Read Message History, Send Messages, Embed Links, Attach Files, Use Slash Commands.
