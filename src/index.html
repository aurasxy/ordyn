Discord Bot

Connect the SOLUS Discord bot to sync ACO orders from your server.

▸ Setup Guide
How to connect SOLUS to your Discord server
Initial Setup
Step 1: Invite the Bot
Click Invite Bot to Server above. Select your Discord server from the dropdown and authorize the bot. You need Manage Server permission on that server.
Step 2: Generate a Link Token
Click Generate Link Token in the Link Status section below. This creates a one-time code that connects your SOLUS license to the bot.
Step 3: Link in Discord
Copy the /link command that appears, go to any channel in your Discord server where the bot is present, paste and send it. The bot will confirm the link.
Step 4: Watch Your ACO Channels
Go to each Discord channel where your ACO bot posts order confirmations and run:
/watch
You can optionally specify the ACO bot type for better parsing:
/watch bot:shikari
Supported bots: Shikari, Stellara, Tohru, Kodai, Refract, or Auto-detect (default)
Getting Orders Into SOLUS
Live Monitoring (New Orders)
Once a channel is watched, the bot automatically captures new orders as your ACO bot posts them. These orders are stored server-side until you pull them into SOLUS.

To pull orders into the app:
- Click Sync Now in the Order Sync section below, or
- Enable Auto-sync to pull orders automatically on a schedule (every 5 min to 1 hour)
Importing Past Orders (/scan)
To import orders that were posted before you set up the bot, go to the ACO channel in Discord and run:
/scan
This scans the last 2,000 messages by default. For deeper history:
/scan limit:50000

The bot will show all detected drops with analytics (stick rates, item breakdowns). From there:
1. Use the dropdown to select any drop and view details
2. Click Export to SOLUS to send that drop's orders
3. Or click Export All to send everything at once
4. Come back here and click Sync Now to pull them into the app
Extra Features
Webhook Forwarding
Forward all captured orders to another Discord channel via webhook:
/forward webhook:https://discord.com/api/webhooks/... title:My Orders
Disable with /forward off:true
Drop Card Themes
Customize the look of drop analytics cards posted from /scan:
/theme
Themes: Midnight, Ocean, Purple, Crimson, Emerald, Slate + custom accent colors
Drop Cards from Scan
After running /scan and selecting a drop, you can:
- Post to Discord — share a styled analytics card to the channel
- Save Image — download the card as a PNG
Bot Commands Reference
/link [token]Link your SOLUS license to the bot /watchStart watching the current channel for ACO orders /watch bot:[name]Watch with a specific parser (shikari, stellara, tohru, kodai, refract) /unwatchStop watching the current channel /scanScan past messages for orders (default: 2,000 messages) /scan limit:[number]Scan more history (max 50,000 messages) /forward webhook:[url]Forward captured orders to a Discord webhook /themeCustomize drop card appearance /statusCheck connection status and watched channels

Only enable if you suspect missing orders. First sync may take 30+ minutes with deep scan on.
